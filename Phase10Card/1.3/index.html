Phase 10 Online Score Card

Select the number of players before you start, you cant change the number after you start it. If you reload the page, click the reload button and it will bring back your old data. If it does something weird, where the whole score card disappears or something like that when you hit reload, you have no saved data, refresh the page and well sorry but its gone.

To use the Dealer Teller, simply set the names of the players in where it says "Player 1" and "Player 2". Then in the dealer order box from top to bottom put the order of dealers at your table according to what the player numbers originally were.
